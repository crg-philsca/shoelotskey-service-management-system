IPO (Input-Process-Output) Mapping

Input Process Output
Customer & Item Info Order Intake: Validates and initializes service records. Order No. & Job Summary
Service Selections Pricing & Scheduling: Computes total and expected date. Full Bill & Timeline
Payment Data Transaction Recording: Logs payment and updates balance. Receipt & Confirmation
Status Updates Workflow Mgt: Tracks progression stages. Live Tracking Dashboard
Release Request Order Fulfillment: Finalizes delivery and item handover. Claimed Record & Item

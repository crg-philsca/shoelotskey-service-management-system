FUNCTIONAL INPUTS

  • Service Intake Data: Brand, Material, Condition Checklist (Sole Sep, Scratches).
  • Order Specifics: Base Service Selection & Dynamic Add-on Quantities.
  • Financial Inputs: Payment Method (GCash/Maya), Partial Amounts, Ref Nos.
  • Logistics: Shipping Preferences (Pickup/Delivery) & Courier Details.

CORE SYSTEM PROCESSES

  • Workflow Management: Orchestrating Status Life-cycle (New → Ongoing → Release).
  • Financial Logic: Grand Total Calculation (Base + Addons) & Revenue Summation.
  • Resource Logic: Staff Task Assignment & Technician Loading.
  • Predictive Engine: Estimating Deadlines based on Service Complexity.

SYSTEM OUTPUTS

  • Dashboard BI: Revenue Trends & Service Volume Groupings (Cleaning/Repair).
  • Audit Logs: Real-time Status History & Transaction Ref Records.
  • Views: Calendar Schedule Manifests & Compact Claim Monitoring Lists.
  • Status Badges: Standardized UI feedback for Priority & Payment states.
